  • Motivation

    Recent trends are showing a drop in the percentage of households donating to charity.This is true across the income spectrum. Patronus is an effort to reverse this trend. We believe streamlining the donations process and providing an incentive for donation will increase donations to people in need.

  • Our Philosophy

    We believe in:


    1. The benefits of donations to charity. We think humans are charitable by nature and want to help others

    2. The adage that "Incentives drive human behavior". Maximizing personal returns will lead to greater charitable donations.

    3. Investing in long-term assets

    4. Investing in quality assets

    5. Passive investment strategies
